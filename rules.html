Challange Rules

1. Any Pokémon that faints must be released or boxed permanently. It is considered “dead” for the rest of the challenge.

2. Only the first wild Pokémon encountered in a route or area can be caught. If the player fails to catch it (ie. it flees or faints), their opportunity to catch a Pokémon in that area is lost.

3. The player must nickname all Pokémon they catch or obtain.

Shiny Clause: If the player encounters a shiny wild Pokémon, they may catch it, even if a wild Pokémon has already been encountered in that area. This does not count as the encounter for that area.

4. You have three attempts to get as far as possible or complete the game.

5. If none of your pokemon can learn a HM needed to progress the story (eg. Surf) you may catch an extra pokemon to learn the HM but you are unable to use it in battle. Consider it a blank slot in your party that just uses HM's.

Winning

The winner is the player who makes it furthest through the challange after three attempts. When you "white out", record your white out location/point in the story (Gym Badge No.) and current playtime.

In the event of a tie location (e.g. both players "white out" at the 3rd gym), the player with the faster time played will be crowned the champion.

VICTORY!

The winner may request a one free meal or drink from every other player that patricipated in the challange and must be complimented on their victory when receiving their reward.
